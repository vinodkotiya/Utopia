Our Story

More than a store.
A living sanctuary.

Founded on a simple vision — to create a warm, judgment-free space where people can smudge, ignite their inner fire, and crystallize their deepest intentions.

"Utopia has been a cornerstone of North Vancouver's spiritual community for two decades, holding space for healing, curiosity, and authentic connection."

Today, under new stewardship that deeply honours this legacy, we continue to hold space for healing crystals, intentional tools, tarot & oracle, and the beautiful creations of local North Vancouver artists and makers.
