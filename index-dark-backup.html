Utopia Store is North Vancouver's trusted metaphysical and crystal shop, serving the spiritual community since 2004. We carry ethically sourced healing crystals, amethyst, rose quartz, obsidian, and selenite, along with tarot and oracle decks, chakra jewelry, singing bowls, smudge tools, orgonite, and handmade pieces from local BC artists. We offer tarot readings, reiki and energy healing, sound baths, and sacred space rental at 1826 Lonsdale Avenue. Our intention kits and crystal gift sets make meaningful spiritual gifts for any occasion. Shop online at shop.utopiastore.ca with free click and collect in North Vancouver. Utopia is also North Vancouver's destination for meaningful spiritual gifts — intention kits, crystal sets, chakra jewelry, and handmade pieces by local artists, perfect for birthdays, housewarmings, grief support, and celebrations of all kinds.
