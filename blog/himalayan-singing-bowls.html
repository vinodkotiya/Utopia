Himalayan Singing Bowls

By Wendy Chivers · January 6, 2024

As we step into a new year, many of us seek ways to cultivate mindfulness and inner peace. One beautiful practice that has been used for centuries is the art of playing Himalayan singing bowls. These ancient instruments produce rich, resonant tones that can help calm the mind, reduce stress, and promote a deep sense of well-being.

Here is a simple seven-step practice you can follow to begin your new year with clarity and calm:

Step 1: Create Your Space

Find a quiet, comfortable spot where you won't be disturbed. Sit on a cushion or chair with your spine straight and your body relaxed. Place the singing bowl on a flat surface or in the palm of your non-dominant hand.

Step 2: Set an Intention

Before you begin, take a moment to set an intention for your practice. This could be a word, a feeling, or a goal you'd like to carry with you into the new year. Hold this intention gently in your mind.

Step 3: Begin with Breath

Close your eyes and take three deep breaths. Inhale slowly through your nose, filling your lungs completely, and exhale through your mouth, releasing any tension or stress.

Step 4: Strike the Bowl

Using a mallet, gently strike the rim of the singing bowl. Listen as the sound emerges and fills the space around you. Allow the vibrations to wash over you, noticing how they feel in your body.

Step 5: Circle the Rim

After the initial strike, slowly run the mallet around the outer rim of the bowl with steady, even pressure. A continuous, singing tone will build. Match your breathing to the rhythm of the sound—slow, steady, and deep.

Step 6: Listen and Absorb

When you feel ready, stop circling and simply listen as the sound gradually fades into silence. Stay present with the vibrations as they dissolve. Notice the stillness that remains.

Step 7: Close Your Practice

Take a few more deep breaths. Gently open your eyes. Recall your intention and carry it with you as you move through your day. Even five minutes with a singing bowl can shift your energy and bring you back to centre.
